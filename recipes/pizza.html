Pickle Pizza

If you love pickles, pizza and ranch; why not combine them and make this delicious pizza

This pizza is topped with Ranch and loads of dill pickles that tastes salty and tangy, you won't be able to spot eating.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Ranch
  • Shredded mozzarella
  • 1 or 2 garlic gloves (thinly sliced)
  • Dried Dill
  • Dill pickle slices

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degree F and place a pizza stone in the middle rack.
  2. Dust a piece of parchment lightly with flour. Stretch or roll out the pizza dough into a 14-inch round on the parchment, then transfer the dough on the parchment to a pizza peel or another inverted rimmed baking sheet.
  3. Spread the ranch and add garlic on the pizza dough and sprinkle with the mozzarella. Top with the pickles.
  4. Slide the pizza on the parchment onto the heated stone and bake until the crust is golden, 12 to 14 minutes. Garnish with dried dill.
